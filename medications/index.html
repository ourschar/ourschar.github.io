About

The medication database is used for quickly retrieving detailed information regarding the usage, adverse reactions, and overdosage information for a specific medication. Simply search by medication name and the information is retrieved from the openFDA database. Do not rely on openFDA to make decisions regarding medical care. Always speak to your health provider about the risks and benefits of FDA-regulated products.
