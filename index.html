About Me

Hi! I'm Charlene Becker, a Front-End Web Developer skilled in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, AJAX, JSON, Twitter Bootstrap and GitHub. I studied at Microsoft Virtual Academy, completing certifications in HTML5 and CSS3, and JavaScript Fundamentals with jQuery. You can find some of the projects I've developed below.

Formerly, I worked at a law firm as a skilled Paralegal in the School Boards and Education sector, where I was the "glue" between attorneys, clients, and the courts. I've also ran my own successful entrepreneurial business in direct sales for 4 years, where I led a sales team of 10, developed marketing strategies, maintained a client database, and attended multiple conferences focusing on leadership, productivity, finance, and best-practices. Life becomes static when you stop learning, and therefore, I was eager to dive head-first into the world of technology and development. So, here I am, and thank you for welcoming me.
